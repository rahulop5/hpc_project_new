METHODOLOGY:
To make gprof work properly on Windows/MinGW:
1. Compiled with '-pg -no-pie -fno-pie' to fix address mapping.
2. For small input sizes, the iteration count was dynamically increased 
   during profiling to ensure the runtime was sufficient (>100ms) for 
   gprof to capture samples.
3. The reported 'Total Execution Time' is normalized to the standard 
   20 iterations.
